Who We Are

Young. Hungry. Bangkok.

Bangkok Elites started with a simple idea: Bangkok needed a rugby club for the next generation. No gatekeeping, no politics — just real rugby and a crew that pushes each other to be better every single session.

Whether you've played your whole life or never touched a ball, if you've got the attitude, you've got a spot.
